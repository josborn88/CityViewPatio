Come to historic Franklinton and enjoy one of the best views of downtown that Columbus has to offer! Located next to the beautiful Scioto Mile City View Patio is in the heart of Columbus. Whether you want to sit and relax, enjoy one of Columbus's many festivals, or take a relaxing walk along the river after a meal, there is something for everyone to enjoy!

We are proud to offer incredible food that supports our planet. We work with local farmers to promote sustainable harvests and offer you seasonal menus. there is alays something new to explore and enjoy at City View!
